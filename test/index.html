Plan your visit

Sample City offers something for every season. Whether you're chasing fall color, summer festivals, or a quiet winter retreat, our guides help you make the most of your time here.

Our visitor centers are open daily and our local concierges can help you book tours, dining reservations, and event tickets — all from a single phone call.

  • Downtown visitor center, 9am–6pm daily
  • Free parking on weekends
  • Trolley tours every 30 minutes
  • Local concierge: 555-VISIT
